Bitcoin

Created by: Satoshi Nakamoto

Released in: 03/01/2009

Bitcoin is an electronic currency that presents new features and stands out for its efficiency, security and ease of exchange.

Its biggest difference compared to other currencies, it is a decentralized currency, so nobody controls it.

It is produced by people and companies from around the world dedicating a large amount of resources to mining.

It does not belong to any State or country and can be used all over the world equally.
